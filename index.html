Car Rental Agreement: Guidelines and Signature Requirements

When it comes to renting a car, following the guidelines in this agreement are essential. From rules for usage to insurance policies, penalties for violating agreements to terms and conditions, this document covers everything you need to know to rent a car.

The Introduction

Welcome!

Thank you for choosing our car rental service. We are excited to help you with your travelling needs and we want to make sure you have a great experience.

Rules for Car Usage

Return Policy:

All rented cars must be returned to the rental location on the date, and at the time detailed in the agreement document.

Driving Age:

The minimum age requirement is 18 years old. However, a higher age may be required based on the type of car rented.

Distances:

The vehicle rented may only be on paved and marked roadways. Unpaved roads or off-roading are not allowed.

Refuelling Policy:

The car must be returned with a full tank of the same fuel type as at the time of renting.

Penalties for Violating Agreement

Smoking Fee:

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the rented car. If we find evidence of smoking, we will charge an extra fee.

Traffic Violation:

You are responsible for any traffic violations that occur during the rental period, and fines or infractions will be charged to your account.

Vehicle Damage Fee:

Any damage found on the car that is not recorded on the damage inspection form will be charged for.

Terms and Conditions

Please read carefully and make sure you fully understand and agree to these terms and conditions before signing the rental agreement.

Reservation Cancellation Policy:

You may cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pick-up time without incurring any additional fees.

Privacy Policy:

We value your privacy and will not disclose any personal information without your consent except as required by law.

Contract Length:

The rental agreement term is detailed in the rental agreement document. The car must be returned on or before the specified date and time.
